This video demonstrates the SPARK Hand, a passive adaptive robotic hand equipped with a Kempe mechanism. It showcases the hand performing three types of grasps: pinching, symmetric scooping, and asymmetric scooping. Various objects, including thin cards and irregular items, are manipulated to highlight the hand’s versatility and adaptability. The video also illustrates the seamless transition between different grasping modes without additional actuators. These demonstrations validate the effectiveness of the SPARK Hand in handling challenging objects and environments.
