1. Overall Description:
This video demonstrates the operation of the SPARK Hand, a passive adaptive robotic hand with a Kempe mechanism. The video shows three types of grasps: pinching, symmetric scooping, and asymmetric scooping, using a variety of objects such as cards, screws, and oranges. The transitions between grasping modes and the hand's adaptability to different shapes and environments are highlighted.

2. Multimedia Player Information:
The video is in MP4 format. It can be played with common media players such as VLC Media Player, Windows Media Player, or QuickTime Player.
